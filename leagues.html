Built for Everyone in the League

Designed for every role on game day and beyond.

League Administrators

One platform. Every team. Every game.

One platform to manage film, stats, schedules, and player data across every team in your league. Standardize the experience, reduce operational overhead, and give every team access to the same quality of tools.

Coaching Staffs

Every game. Fully broken down. No extra work.

Every team in the league gets fully broken down film for every game — not just their own. Scout opponents, review your own performance, and prepare with professional-grade film without any extra work from your staff.

Game Day Operations

From replay operators to officials.

ScoreBreak streamlines game day from every angle — Airborne Stadium delivers frame-accurate multi-angle replay for officiating review, and replay operators get a reliable system that integrates with your existing video infrastructure.

Recruiting

Every game is a recruiting opportunity.

League stats sites are publicly accessible — college coaches and scouts can view standings, player profiles, stats, and watch the film behind the numbers. Every game in your league becomes a recruiting opportunity for every player.

Film Exchange

Every team in the league. On the same page.

ScoreBreak's Film Exchange gives leagues a centralized hub for video distribution. Whether teams use ScoreBreak or another platform, everyone stays connected to the same film.

Universal Access

Teams using any platform can access and download league film through the Exchange. No ScoreBreak subscription required for exchange-only access.

Broken Down Film

ScoreBreak teams get fully broken down film — professionally tagged and statted via Handoff, or broken down by the teams themselves. All breakdowns flow into the same league hub.

Fully Standardized

When the whole league is on ScoreBreak, everyone works from the same film. Same breakdowns. One platform for scouting, prep, and review.

How ScoreBreak Portal Works

Coaches subscribe. Athletes get profiles. College coaches discover talent.

Automatic Recruiting Profiles

Every athlete on a subscribed team automatically gets a complete recruiting profile built from their film and highlights — no individual sign-up required.

Athlete Clip Folders

Athletes clip their own plays directly from game film and organize them into categorized folders — by play type, position, or situation. College coaches get a curated, easy-to-navigate view of exactly what they care about most.

Full Game Film Access

College coaches get direct access to complete game footage pulled from the team's film library — not just highlight clips, but every play in context.

College Coach Discovery

College coaches search and browse athlete profiles across subscribed programs — everything they need to evaluate prospects in one place.

Highlight Reels

Automatically compiled from tagged plays in ScoreBreak software — a curated reel of each athlete's best moments, always up to date.

Share Profiles Anywhere

Athletes can share their ScoreBreak profile with any college coach via a simple link — no login required. Coaches outside the platform get instant access to film, highlights, and academic records.

Platform

Purpose-Built for Coaches, Players, and Leagues

Every feature of ScoreBreak is designed specifically for the lacrosse ecosystem — from grassroots recruiting to elite competition.

Athlete Profiles

Complete player profiles with highlights, stats, and game footage — automatically built and kept current as your team's film is broken down.

Scouting

Powerful search and filtering tools let coaches find the exact recruits they need, filtered by position, class year, academics, and more.

Integrated Hardware

Airborne™ sideline replay connects directly to ScoreBreak — no extra setup, no workarounds. The same system covers away games, home games, and practice.

Game Film Analysis

Tag, clip, and annotate plays with custom tag boards built for lacrosse. Share film packages with your staff and players to accelerate game prep and review.

Team Preparation

Build scouting reports and opponent breakdowns — with expert Handoff analysis, your coaching staff focuses on execution while ScoreBreak handles the film work.

Effortless Communication

Keep athletes, coaches, and parents connected with integrated messaging and automatic notifications — film, feedback, and communication all in one place.

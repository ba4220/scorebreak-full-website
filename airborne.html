The Technology

What is Airborne?

Airborne is ScoreBreak's local network video replay system — a purpose-built hardware and software solution that brings instant replay to any sideline, anywhere.

Unlike cloud-based systems, Airborne operates entirely on its own local wireless network. No internet connection required, no latency from external servers — just fast, reliable replay that coaches and officials can count on when every second matters.

No internet required — runs on its own local network
Integrated live stats feed directly in the replay interface
Multi-camera support for complete field coverage
Works at any venue — from practice fields to major stadiums
Deeply connected to ScoreBreak coaching software

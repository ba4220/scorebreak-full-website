Our Mission

Connecting athletes, coaches, and leagues through technology

ScoreBreak was founded with a simple belief: every athlete deserves the tools to reach their potential, every coach deserves the intelligence to lead their team to victory, and every league deserves the infrastructure to grow.

We built the only platform that addresses all three — seamlessly connecting recruiting, game film, sideline replay, and league management into a single, intuitive experience.

Today, ScoreBreak serves lacrosse programs across the country and is expanding to football through ScoreBreak Gridiron, with more sports on the horizon.

What We Believe

Built on principles that matter

Our values guide every product decision, every feature, and every interaction with our community.

01

Athletes First

Every feature we build starts with a question: how does this help athletes reach their potential? When athletes succeed, coaches succeed, and programs grow.

02

Intelligence Wins Games

The best-prepared teams win more often than the most talented ones. We give coaches the data, film tools, and insights to prepare their teams better than anyone.

03

Technology Should Just Work

Sideline replay that sets up in minutes. Film tools that are intuitive. Profiles that coaches can actually find. If technology is a burden, we've failed. We build for simplicity.

04

The Whole Game, Not Just Part of It

From the first recruiting profile to the last second of a championship — we build tools for the entire sport lifecycle, not just one piece of it.

05

Real Support, Real People

Sports happen on weekends, at night, at away games. We show up when it counts — with fast, expert support from people who understand the product and the game.

06

Growing the Game

Lacrosse, football, and beyond — we're committed to growing every sport we serve by connecting more athletes, empowering more coaches, and making leagues better.

Our Story

Built for the Coaches Who Demand More.

ScoreBreak is the sports technology company connecting athletes with coaches, giving teams the intelligence to outperform opponents, and providing professional organizations with game-changing solutions.

Our Founding

Founded on the Sideline.

ScoreBreak was founded in 2015 by Jordan Hendry — a retired professional lacrosse player for the Denver Outlaws and former collegiate athlete at the University of Denver. Jordan built ScoreBreak because he lived the problem firsthand: coaches needed better tools, faster film, and real intelligence on the sideline and in the film room.

What started as a solution for lacrosse has grown into a complete sports technology platform serving programs across lacrosse, basketball, and football.

Our Mission

Professional tools. Built for everyone.

ScoreBreak is designed as a pro-sumer platform — professional-grade technology that any coach, at any level, can pick up and use. From a first-year high school coach to a Division 1 program with a full staff, ScoreBreak meets you where you are. We believe every coaching staff deserves access to the same quality of tools that the pros use. That belief drives every product decision we make.

Where We Play

Serving coaches across every sport.

Lacrosse

Where it all started.

ScoreBreak was built in lacrosse and remains the dominant platform in the sport — trusted by the majority of Division 1 programs. From film analysis and sideline replay to recruiting and league solutions, ScoreBreak powers lacrosse at every level.

Basketball

From high school to the pros.

ScoreBreak serves basketball programs from high school through the professional ranks — including the Unrivaled league. Film analysis, officiating replay, and sideline replay built for the pace and complexity of the modern game.

Football — ScoreBreak Gridiron

Built for the game that deserves better.

High school football deserves better. ScoreBreak Gridiron is our answer — a modern alternative built from the ground up for football coaches who want powerful film analysis, reliable sideline replay, and automated in-game reporting. Launching soon.
